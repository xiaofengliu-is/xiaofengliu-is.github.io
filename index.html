Teaching

Teaching Demo:

 

  • Radiant Tutorial Series:
    • I designed and created a series of 28 technical tutorial videos for Radiant, an open-source platform-independent browser-based interface for business analytics in R
    • Topics cover decision analysis, probability basics, hypothesis testing, linear regression analysis, and simulation.
    • Each video contains examples and reproducible code.
    • It is a key support material for the classes using Radiant and overall Radiant community.
